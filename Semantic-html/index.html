DEEPDIVE INTO ICT 2022 MENTORSHIP

Welcome to the DEEPDIVE INTO ICT 2022 Mentorship Program.
The 2022 mentorship presents the easiest trading model for beginner traders as well as seasoned traders looking to simplify their systems.

Program Overview

  • Duration: The mentorship program runs for a period of 6 months.
  • Mentor-Mentee Matching: Mentees will be matched with mentors based on their interests, goals, and expertise.
  • Meetings: Regular meetings (virtual or in-person) will be scheduled between mentors and mentees to discuss progress, challenges, and opportunities.
  • Workshops and Webinars: Participants will have access to exclusive workshops and webinars on various ICT topics.
  • Networking Opportunities: The program will facilitate networking events to connect mentees with industry professionals.

How to Get Involved

If you are interested in becoming a mentor or mentee, please fill out the application form available on our website. We encourage individuals from diverse backgrounds and experiences to apply.

If you have any questions or need further information about the DEEPDIVE INTO ICT 2022 Mentorship Program, please contact us.
